Network Administrator
Qualification and Requirements

Education

A Network administrator is a person who is responsible for setting up,maintaing,upgrading and configuring virus protection on a existing computer network. They ensure that the network is running properly eleminating problems if they arise.
The education level that is required of an individual to become a Network Administrator is the minimum, a Bachelor in a computer related study such as Information Technology, Networking and Computer Security.

Skills and Certification

Certification is not manditory as a network administrator,but some may require certification such as CompTIA A+, CompTIA Network+ and LPI Linux Essentials.The position of a Network Administrator requires a diverse skill set, some of these include coding, problem-solving,attention to details,technical skills, this list is by no means exhaustive.

Personal Development

Personal Development


“Twelve Pillars” by Jim Rohn and Chris Widener

Applying it to my life

In the chapters 4 to 7 of “Twelve Pillars” by Jim Rohn and Chris Widener, these were about goals, surrounding yourself with the best people and continuous learning. The conversation between Charlie and Michael revolves around good habits for personal development. It is advised that to move forward in life you should have your goals written down, this helps you to realize and remember what you want to achieve. I could use this in my life because I currently haven’t put much thought to goals in life or achieving them. This taught me that I can also break down my goals from short term to long-term making it easier for achievement. In another conversation Charlie again advised Michael of how his time and his social circle can affect how he has been perceiving things and his advancement in life. This helped me to realized that if you only have friends that are bound to one place or have no future this will also influence your attitude. I could apply this to my life by beginning to weed out the friends or associates who are not benefiting me and helping me to kill my potential. I should also look into building my self first and then I may attract others who want the same mindset as me. Charlie also taught Michael that learning never stop once you grow older. In orders to grow and become successful self-education is the key and can be done through reading, learning from others and reflection. I can apply this point to my life as I am not much of a reader and reading open your mind to vast new information. From these chapters in the book the message is clearly stated strategic goal setting, positive associations and continuous learning are fundamental elements in growing and achieving a successful life.
